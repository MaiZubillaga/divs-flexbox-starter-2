Hi. I'm Mai

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the rabit hole of blogs & books, I can be found travelling, hiking and searching for new stories.

About me: I was born and raised in the Basque Country, North of Spain. When people think about Spain, they immediately think about Madrid or Barcelona, but you're missing out if you visit the Iberian Peninsula and don't stop in the Basque Country!

My passion: I love traveling, and the beach is my favorite destination. Until a few years ago, I thought I was a city gal, but I also enjoy the peace and quiet of a small town. I've been getting into reading again. My goal for this year is to read 40 books!

A fun fact: In January 2014 I landed in the US for the first time. I left all my friends and family behind, only bringing a big suitcase and a carry on. I was coming just for a year, but life would have it that I ended up staying for 8. And counting!
